      Primary PASC branches

      • CaseReport -> Patient -> Episode anchors the case.
      • Patient -> BackgroundContext owns pre/post context.
      • Episode -> Exposure -> Drug owns medication use.

      Conditional branches

      • Acute COVID is a contextual Episode that precedes Long COVID.
      • Pregnancy is a contextual Episode that overlaps Long COVID.
      • Vaccination hangs from the acute COVID episode through VaccinationEvent.
      • Alternative therapies hang from the Long COVID episode through Therapy.
